About In Depth

Rooted in Bolton.
Committed to everyone.

We're a Bolton-based inclusive recruitment consultancy that believes the best workplaces are built on diversity of thought, background, and experience. We partner with employers who are ready to do the work — and candidates who deserve a fair shot.

Our approach goes beyond ticking boxes. We embed inclusion at every stage of the hiring process, from job design through to onboarding, so that every hire sticks — and thrives.

Honest and straightforward

No jargon, no corporate fluff. We tell it as it is and give you real, actionable advice.

People, not pipelines

We see candidates as full human beings, not resources to be processed.

Proudly northern

Bolton born, North West focused — we know this region and its talent inside out.
